Data Science Africa summer school is aimed at equipping participants with Machine Leaning, Data Science and Artificial Intelligence skills. This will be organised in sesions and after each session there will be exercises to assess the learning.

To this end, we require that participants are well versed with the basics of the technologies and languages that will be used in the summer school. Particularly, we want to make sure participants have sufficient base skills in Python programming, Data science and Machine learning. You are required to download the notebook from the link below (by clicking it), complete the notebook and then fill out the registration form below that requires you to upload the completed notebook. Attendance to the summer school will be based on your performance in this notebook.
